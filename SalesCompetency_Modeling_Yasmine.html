Project Summary

Comprehensive competency modeling for sales role differentiation

This project developed a complete competency framework to differentiate between Hunter (new business acquisition) and Farmer (account management) sales roles. The framework includes 12 operationally-defined competencies, 4-level proficiency scales, behavioral indicators, and validated psychometric measures.

Measurement Scales Overview

Psychometric instruments used in the competency assessment

Six validated psychometric scales were developed to measure the 12 competencies. Each scale includes 6-10 items rated on a 5-point Likert scale (1 = Strongly Disagree to 5 = Strongly Agree). All scales demonstrate strong internal consistency (α > 0.80) and test-retest reliability.

Convergent & Discriminant Validity

Factor structure and scale independence

Confirmatory factor analysis supports the 3-factor structure (Hunter, Farmer, Universal competencies). Factor loadings range from 0.62 to 0.89, with adequate model fit: CFI = 0.94, RMSEA = 0.06, SRMR = 0.05.
